Girls Who Code


I began designing for Girls Who Code in March of 2019. This entailed designing the layout of the Girls Who Code sponsorship package as well as the cover for the package. It helped me realize the impact a good layout can have for potential stakeholders.

Asian Outlook


As layout editor of this literary magazine at Binghamton University, I was able to read articles written by the student body and tailor the designs around the topic at hand.

Binghamton Student Design Agency


At this internship, I improved my ability to handle several projects at a time. It has been a wonderful time designing posters, logos and other branding materials for clubs who are Student Association chartered at Binghamton University.

The Phoenix


As co-layout editor of The Phoenix of 2017, I helped create a magazine of 156 glossy pages. This contained specially selected pieces from the talented students of Townsend Harris High School, ranging from fictional pieces, poems, photographs, collages and artwork. This won a magazine crown from the Columbia Scholastic Press Association.
